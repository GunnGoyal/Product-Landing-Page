HOW DO I RESET MY NIKE PASSWORD?

On mobile devices, tap the menu in the upper right corner. Scroll down and tap "Sign In." On a computer, click "Sign In" in the upper right corner. Enter the email address connected to your Nike Member profile and tap or click "Continue." Tap or click "Forgot Password?" We’ll email you a one-time verification code—use the code to reset your password.

WHERE IS MY NIKE ORDER?

As a Nike Member, you can easily check the status of your order by signing in to your profile and heading to your order history. You also can download or open the Nike App to track your order. Once your order has shipped, you can see which carrier is delivering your packages and track shipments along the way. Here’s how: Go to your orders page. Tap the specific item within the order you want to track. Tap the "Track Shipment" button to see the estimated delivery date along with the carrier delivering it and the tracking number.
